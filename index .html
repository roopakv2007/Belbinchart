BELBIN TEAM ROLES

Belbin's team roles theory, developed by R. Meredith Belbin, categorizes nine types of behavior that people exhibit in a team setting. The theory posits that a team's success is based on a balance of these behaviors, rather than individual intelligence.

THE NINE BELBIN TEAM ROLES

Understanding the Three Role Categories

Action-Oriented Roles
Drive the team forward and ensure tasks get completed with precision and efficiency
Thought-Oriented Roles
Provide strategy, analysis, and creative solutions through deep thinking and expertise
People-Oriented Roles
Foster collaboration, communication, and team cohesion for a harmonious environment
